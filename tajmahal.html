Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in India. It is located in Agra and was built by Emperor Shah Jahan.

Commissioned in 1631 by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, it honors his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Overwhelmed with grief, Emperor Shah Jahan began construction on the mausoleum in 1632 to serve as her final resting place.

The main structure was completed in 1648, and the surrounding gardens, mosque, and gateways were finalized in 1653.

Today, it stands not just as a tomb, sbut as an enduring and spectacular symbol of undying love.
